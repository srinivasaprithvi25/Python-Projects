Tumor Types

Understanding the nature of brain tumors is crucial for diagnosis and treatment. Below are some of the most common types.

Giloma

Gliomas are brain tumors originating from glial cells, ranging from benign to malignant. Symptoms vary by location and include headaches and seizures.

Learn More

Meningioma

Meningiomas are typically benign tumors arising from the meninges, causing symptoms like headaches and vision issues. Treatment usually involves surgical removal.

Learn More

Pituitary

Pituitary tumors affect hormone production from the pituitary gland, leading to hormonal imbalances and vision problems. They are often treated with surgery, medication, and radiation.
